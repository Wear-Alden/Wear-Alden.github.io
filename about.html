About Christopher Alden

Christopher Alden is more than just underwear — it's a statement about how luxury, comfort, and real bodies come together. Founded with the belief that everyone deserves to feel confident in what they wear underneath, we focus on creating thoughtfully designed pieces with premium fabric, honest pricing, and bold-but-minimal aesthetics.

Designed in the Pacific Northwest, every piece reflects our commitment to comfort and inclusivity. Whether you're dressing for confidence, expression, or comfort, Christopher Alden is made for you.
